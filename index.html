Flowers

Roses

Roses are known as a symbol of love.
They are also called by their scientific name Rosa, roses have over three hundred species. Not only are they one of the most wide-known flower type but also one of the oldest species of plant cultivated and used for decorations.

Tulips

Tulips are a known symbol of happiness and joy. Tulips are also called by their scientific name Tulipa. Holland is the largest producer of tulips worldwide, exporting about 3 billion bulbs per year. They also come in the wide range of colors from red to purple, blue and even black.

Lilies

Lilies are known as the symbol of purity and beauty. Lilies are also called by their scientific name Lilium. They also have one of the longest in-vase lifespans of any cut bloom, but be careful because they are poisonous to cats!

Sunflowers

Sunflowers are as the symbol for loyalty and adoration. Sunflowers are also called by their scientific name Helianthus. They are a great healthy snack, their seeds are a rich source of protein, potassium, magnesium, calcium, iron, and vitamin E. Not only that, sunflowers are unique, they need a lot of sun (around 6-8 hours a day) so the buds rotate towards the sun to get the most amount as possible.
